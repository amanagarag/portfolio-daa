Project 1: Suryapath

Suryapath is a vibrant and modern city located in the state of Maharashtra, India, approximately 1500 kilometers from major urban hubs. Nestled near the historic city of Kolhapur, Suryapath blends cultural heritage with contemporary development. Renowned for its lush green surroundings, the city boasts advanced infrastructure, sustainable urban planning, and a strong focus on renewable energy. Its name, inspired by the sun, reflects the city's emphasis on solar-powered initiatives and eco-friendly practices. With a harmonious mix of tradition and innovation, Suryapath is emerging as a beacon of progress and a model for smart city development in India.
